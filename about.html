About Me

I'm from Lehi, Utah and currently attend school at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. My parents are both from Payson, Utah and have been married for almost 28 years. I have four brothers and one sister. I am the third child.

Following my graduation from Skyridge High School in 2020 I left home to serve a two year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I spent 17 of my 24 months in St. Louis, Missouri and my last 7 months in Ecuador.

I am determined, hard-working, ambitious, faithful and loyal. My life goals include faithfully living the gospel of Jesus Christ, providing for my future family, and serving others.
